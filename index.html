Forecasting Formula: CAGR

Compound Annual Growth Rate is used to determine the average growth rate over a period.

\[ CAGR = \left( \frac{\text{Ending Value}}{\text{Beginning Value}} \right)^{\frac{1}{\text{n}}} - 1 \]

Where 'n' is the number of compounding periods.
