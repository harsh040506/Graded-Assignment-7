Forecasting Formula

Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR)

\[ CAGR = \left( \frac{\text{Ending Value}}{\text{Beginning Value}} \right)^{\frac{1}{\text{n}}} - 1 \]

Where 'n' is the number of years.

Explain that CAGR provides a smoothed-out average growth rate. Mention that we will use this to project next year's performance.
